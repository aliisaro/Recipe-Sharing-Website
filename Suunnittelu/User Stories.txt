

1.As a user, I want to create a new recipe entry:

Acceptance Criteria:
Include fields for recipe name, ingredients, preparation steps, and cooking time.
Allow users to add optional details such as difficulty level and cuisine type.
Provide an option to upload images of the dish.



2.As a user, I want to search for recipes:

Acceptance Criteria:
Enable searching by recipe name, ingredients, cuisine, or difficulty level.
Provide filters to narrow down search results.



3.As a user, I want to view details of a recipe:

Acceptance Criteria:
Display the recipe name, ingredients, preparation steps, cooking time, and any additional details.
Include an option to view images related to the recipe.



4.As a user, I want to like and comment on recipes:

Acceptance Criteria:
Allow users to express appreciation by liking recipes.
Enable users to leave comments and ask questions about specific recipes.



5.As a user, I want to save my favorite recipes:

Acceptance Criteria:
Provide a "Save" or "Favorite" button on each recipe page.
Allow users to access a list of their saved recipes for quick reference.




6.As a user, I want to share my recipes with others:

Acceptance Criteria:
Include a share button on each recipe page to share the recipe on social media or through a link.
Ensure that shared recipes are accessible even to non-app users.




7.As a user, I want to follow other users:

Acceptance Criteria:
Allow users to follow and be followed by other users.
Provide a feed that shows recipes from users I follow.



8.As a user, I want to edit or delete my recipes:

Acceptance Criteria:
Allow users to make changes to their recipes, including updating ingredients, steps, or images.
Provide an option to delete a recipe.
